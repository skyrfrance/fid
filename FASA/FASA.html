Florida Artists Support Association

Welcome!

We are the Florida Artists Support Association, also known as FASA. We are a group of creators living and working in the state of Florida that bring awareness to each other's work such as pieces in shows, street art, and murals in the area. We pride ourselves on providing financial support in the process so they can keep creating. We highlight beautiful works of art all throughout the beautiful Sunshine State and provide artists a space where they can keep making the world more colorful.
